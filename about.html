Our Story

The Beginning

MISTRESSA was born in a small atelier tucked away in the hills of Grasse, France — the world's perfume capital. Founder Elara Voss, a third-generation perfumer, set out to create fragrances that tell stories rather than follow formulas.

Each MISTRESSA fragrance begins with a single emotion — a memory of rain on summer stone, the quiet before dawn, the warmth of velvet against skin. From that spark, rare ingredients are sourced from small cooperatives around the world, ensuring both exceptional quality and ethical practices.

Our Philosophy

We believe perfume is not an accessory — it is an extension of identity. Our compositions are built to evolve on the skin, revealing different facets throughout the day. We use only the finest natural extracts, aged like fine wine, and never compromise on concentration.

Every bottle is hand-filled, individually numbered, and sealed with a ribbon woven in a small Italian workshop that has supplied silk ribbons since 1892. This is not mass production. This is craftsmanship.
